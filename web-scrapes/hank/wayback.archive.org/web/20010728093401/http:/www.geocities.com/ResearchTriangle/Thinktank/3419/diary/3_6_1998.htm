Hello all!
This truly will be a quick note, but I just wanted to tell you all about yesterday's goings on. Yesterday was a short day because of a Frankfurt only holiday called Wäldchestag. This day was traditionally set aside so that everyone could literally go take a walk in the woods. In the woods, they would find a very nice place to set up and have a party. Today, they really do it up with a carnival in the Frankfurt Staadtwald. It is nice for everyone to get away from their jobs together and go to the woods. I went with quite a few (about 10) colleagues from work. It was a lot of fun with Apfelwein and bier drinking for everyone. There was wonderful food and we all went on the Ferris Wheel to see the Frankfurter skyline. It was a very beautiful night and fun was had by all. We left the office around 14:00-14:30 and got there by 15:00. We stayed (all of us) until 23:00. It was a great time filled with comraderie and laughter. I think this is a team building experience that all companies should employ. Well, needless to say, when I got home at about 11:30 I went straight to bed. I was dog tired, but it was worth it.
Well, that is my news. Let me hear from you.

Take care,
Hank

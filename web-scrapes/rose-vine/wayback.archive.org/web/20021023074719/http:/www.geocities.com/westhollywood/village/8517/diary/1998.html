1998 Entries

 

January 17, 1998
I have decide to start writing again. I stopped due to school and other reasons, but I thought I needed to write today since it has been so long. I still haven't told anyone and I doubt I will soon unless it is a matter of life or death. I am still in the same bind I have been in since the last time I wrote. When I really think about it at night, I can't help but cry. I fell like I am stranded on an island with no way off. Nothing will rescue me and no boats are near. But with me it is my body. I am stuck in it, no way out. No one can save me since they don't know about me and I can't tell them. When and if I am finally helped, surgery is my boat off of the Island. I guess I will never know what my future holds but I hope this wait is worth it.

January 24, 1998
I am slowly becoming more at ease in the public with myself being a transsexual, not dressing, but mentally. Every night while writing this journal, I find it helps in different ways but also gets me thinking which makes me more sad and depressed. This journal has brought up so many thoughts and memories since I started. It helps me find the root of my transsexualism. The one goal for this journal now is to someday write, "tomorrow is the big day, SRS!"

May 17, 1998
It is now storming outside tonight (actually morning at 12:24 am). The storm is easy to relate to since my emotions are storming as well. It is pretty much final that my parents are going to get a divorce which just complicates things more. I will be glad when this mess clears up, my parents live happily without fights, and I become the woman I know I am.

May 20, 1998
I do not know why depression has to come and go. I would rather be somewhat depressed all the time than live a roller coaster of emotions. I never no what will happen next, sometimes it is so bad all I do is sit in my room thinking about various things. Myself, my future, why I am in this situation. And the worst part about it is I have no one to turn to personally. Yes, I have e-mail friends, but can I physically hug them? Can they help me by face to face conversations? No, Michelle is honest and intelligent but even she can't help me like my parents can. There are not enough words to describe what I am going through.

November 14, 1998
It has been awhile since I last wrote. So you can easily see nothing major has happened. I have met a very nice woman in California named Candice. She has helped me a lot and is much like a second mom.

My e-mail is getting cluttered with mail from people who adore my web page. So far I have not had any threats or insulting letters, and I hope I never do. I never know what I will get next. I wish someone would write with a cure but I need to take my first "baby" steps towards, well actually away, from the hell that I am embedded in . The male poison is crawling closer to my heart. No one know when it will tear my sane feminine mind apart and leave a confused male to die. Trying to cope with my male body is not worth the fight. I am female. Just as Malcom X believed complete separation from whites is how it should be, I am fighting for a complete separation from my maleness. Although leaving behind my past is not possible, gaining a new future is.

I got a fortune cookie today, the fortune and lucky numbers were:

Your dearest wish will come true.

4 23 26 38 36 39

I hope it is right this time. A change for the better now would really help

1997 Entries

 

June 16, 1997
I am becoming more comfortable talking with friends on the phone while be the girl I am. I don't have that guilty feeling anymore. The feeling that it's not me (the physical me) they're talking to, it's Sarah. I am glad that feeling is going away.

I decided on using Sarah as my name. I found out it means lady/princess. I am a lady but want to look like a princess.

I found a costume wig today that I hope my mom will put in the attic so I can take it. It may not be the best but it will work at least until I tell my parents which I am not sure of when and if I will.

June 29, 1997
I am hoping my parents will leave me alone for a while sometime soon. I had two and a half hours today but found that was not enough since I didn't know when exactly my parents were going to get home. When I get my drivers license it will become easier to get supplies and become the woman that I am. My hope is that I can go around town as Sarah soon. I suppose though that I will have to get through high school (and perhaps college) before I can make the transition permanently. I very much would like to be a woman. I hope that SRS technology will improve by the time I go ahead with it so I can be as feminine as I possibly can be.

July 3, 1997
Today I had the closest call with being caught as a woman. I saw my moms car pull away and thought I was alone, so I got out of bed and went down stairs (with a night gown on). I decided to check my moms room just in case and she was there. I was lucky that she was sleeping.

July 6, 1997
I was at Kings Dominion (a theme park near where I live) yesterday, and while I was there I thought some more about myself. I discovered more to my transsexualism while I was there. While thinking I couldn't picture myself as a husband or father, the only role I fit into was wife or mother. I wish I could be saved from this male body soon.

July 10, 1997
Today I may have figured out why I may be a transsexual. My mom has always favored my brother and many times she says she wants a girl. I don't know if that caused it but I guess it may have.

July 12, 1997
I was at a friends yesterday, another hard night of not being myself. While there I was able to think a little. We had to drop off my friends sister (lucky she is to be a girl). She was going somewhere with ten other girls. When I was thinking, I realized when I looked at those girls that I really wanted to exchange places with them.

Girls are the luckiest sex on earth, why must I be a boy?

July 15, 1997
Today was the first time I told anyone about myself. Even though it wasn't much, I did it. I posted a message on the internet telling people about me. This is just the beginning of my transition.

July 17, 1997
Today was the first time I had ever talked to a friend in person while wearing panties. It happened by accident since I didn't know he was coming. I am very seriously thinking about telling my parents (mom first). I hope they understand and will support me rather than hate me. I will hopefully find out how to tell them, I only have one chance.

July 19, 1997
It is getting harder to write everyday unless something extraordinary happens. Today nothing of that occurred while I was myself, the girl I love so much. It is also getting harder to keep this all a secret. The more I want to be me, the more I do to get as close to Sarah as I can. I might as well tell my mom so she won't find out first.

July 20, 1997
A little over three months until I get my drivers license. Hopefully I will have told my parents by then and I will have no need to have to continue on getting supplies secretly. I very much need my parents support.

July 22, 1997
Right now I feel that just dressing like a woman will get me nowhere and that it is time to tell my parents. Hopefully my courage will come and I will do what I need to and make the next step of my journey.

July 23, 1997
The image of me as a female in the future is stronger now. It is harder to make it through the day without wishing and wanting to be a female physically. It is very hard now to picture me the man in a relationship. I always see myself as a girl. Every woman I see, I picture myself as that woman. There is no way I can stop that until I am the female who I am.

July 25, 1997
I went to a TS girls home page today. Her name is Amanda. I e-mailed her in hope of finding a way to tell my parents. She too, was in this same situation. Right now I am home alone wearing a slip, panty hose and panties and a bra; I was wearing a flowery dress. Now, even dressing like a woman is not enough to comfort the pain I am going through, the pain of being a man. No words can describe how I am feeling. I don't know how I will get through another year of school while being a male.

July 28, 1997
I got a reply from Amanda today. It had a few helpful tips but not enough. She said not to tell my parents unless it is a matter of life or death. Right now it is not but it could be soon. I very much want SRS but I am not the type of person who would kill myself for something. Maybe that will change and then I will have to tell my parents.

July 29, 1997
I replied to Amanda today. I really hope she can help me and relieve some of my stress. I found out she was forced to tell her parents, I think this is the best way to go but I have no clue how to get my parents to force it out of me.

August 3, 1997
I forgot to write yesterday, oops. It doesn't really matter anyway. My friend came and I had to act male again. I could never imagine me and any of my friends getting married to each other. I might be able to after my transformation, but not now. Now I can see me and some man, but not my friends. I am still thinking about how to tell my parents and continue on into femininity. I am almost positive my mom will support me.

August 5, 1997
I have started writing every other day now, or unless something big happens. Depression is overcoming me at night when I think about me, and I think more during the day. It used to be just sometimes during the night when I was depressed. Now it is almost all night and some during the day. I would like to kill off my male side and just leave Sarah but that is impossible. I don't want to die.

August 7, 1997
Today is the last night I will be here before I leave for Jekyll Island, Georgia for vacation. I will continue to be Sarah at night there. I will have to be careful not to be caught though. Right now, however, I kind of want to be caught.

August 13, 1997
Why must there be separation of the races, or sexes? This world could, and would, be much better, a better place for all. An even better question is why some people can't accept others for who they are? Transsexuals could talk to others (non-transsexuals) freely and not worry. I could tell my parents about who I really am. And maybe even people who think of suicide can talk to someone and not worry about getting in trouble. I wish I could change all that, I wish I could become who I am.

August 15, 1997
Why must I be put through this hell? Not only am I deprived of being who I really am, it seems as though my family hates me also. I really do not want to die. I wish there was something to do. This is my last night in Jekyll. I like the change in scenery here as Sarah, even though it is just a room change. I haven't gotten caught yet, hopefully I can keep it that way unless I tell it is better for me to have them know.
